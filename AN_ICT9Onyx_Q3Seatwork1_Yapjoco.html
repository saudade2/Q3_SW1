One Piece

Synopsis

One Piece is an animanga focusing on Monkey D. Luffy, who when he ate a devil fruit became a man of rubber. Inspired by a very powerful pirate called "Shanks", he set off on an adventure to find the "One Piece", which is a collection of treasures of unimaginable value, if collected, the person is seen as the "Pirate King". Along the way, he meets a variety of characters, some bad and some good.

Characters



Monkey D. Luffy

Voice Actor: Tanaka, Mayumi

Monkey D. Luffy or Luffy in short, is the main protagonist of One Piece. After getting inspired by Shanks, he set off on a journey to find the "One Piece" treasure and become the Pirate King. Along the way he meets people who became his allies and became members of his crew called "The Straw Hat Pirates".



Roronoa, Zoro

Voice Actor: Nakai, Kazuya

Zoro Roronao or Zoro is the right-hand man of his crew's captain Luffy. Zoro is a master of the Three Sword Style, which makes him wield 3 swords, he is one of the best swordsman in One Piece. His goal is to become the greatest swordsman in the world after a promise to his dead childhood friend.


Vinsmoke Sanji

Voice Actor: Hirata, Hiroaki

Vinsmoke Sanji or Sanji is the main cook and the left-hand man of the Straw Hat Pirates. He is characterized by his superb cooking skills and unique fighting style, which is the black leg style. His goal is to find the all blue, which is a legendary ocean which contains all the fish in the world



Tony Tony Chopper

Voice Actor: Ootani, Ikue

Tony Tony Chopper or Chopper is the doctor of the Straw Hat Pirates. Chopper is a reindeer, but has the power to change into a human hybrid. His goal is to be able to cure any disease in the world.



Nami

Voice Actor: Okamura, Akemi

Nami is the navigator of the Straw Hat Pirates. She is characterized as being one of the smartest and best navigators in One Piece, as well as her personality being cunning and bossy. Her goal is to draw a map of the entire world in One Piece.
